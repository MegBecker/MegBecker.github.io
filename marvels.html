All of the Marvels

Douglas Wolk read all—yes, you read that correctly—of the Marvel comics and lived to tell the tale. And what a tale it is: All of the Marvels is a sweeping analysis and helpful guide into the realm of comics and culture since their conception. I worked closely with the marketing team to create assets for socials and targeting advertising, using assets from the jacket art along with custom illustrations and designs.

Jacket designed by Will Staehle. Designed at Penguin Random House.
