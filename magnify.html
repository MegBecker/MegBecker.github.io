Magnify Conference

Magnify Conference is designed to fill the gap between women’s ministry and networking, creating an environment for women to dream and talk about their work through the lens of faith. I designed the logo and visuals, and collaborated with Alaray Creative and Vivid on the conference itself.

It was important to us that the conference feel bold, fresh, and inclusive. The colors, shapes, and patterns represent the unique skills that each person will bring to the conference, and the overlapping and in-motion feel of the design highlights the collaborative nature of the conference, as ideas are shared and new things are created and discovered.
