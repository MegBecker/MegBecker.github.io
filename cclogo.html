Carolina Cruisers

The Carolina Cruisers are an adaptive track team for people of all ages and phyiscal abilities. While not all participants use a wheelchair, we developed a recognizable racing figure for a primary mark that would read easily on a shirt. A website was developed along with the logo, and their partnership with the Charlotte Hornets informs the colors.
