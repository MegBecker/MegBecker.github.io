The Life of Forms

The Life of Forms was an exhibition by Di Donna Galleries that featured sculptures by Ruth Asawa, Agustín Cárdenas, and Louise Bourgeois, among others. I had the privilege of designing the accompanying catalog for the exhibition, and oversaw print and production for it as well.

Designed at McCall Associates and printed at GHP Media in West Haven, Connecticut.
