Printed materials

Every book is unique, and so are the campaigns designed for them. Sometimes the opportunity arises to create custom print materials—anything from postcards to a book club kit to a mailing box to a record. These materials are shared by authors and often sent out with advance copies of books to build buzz.

Designed at Penguin Random House.
