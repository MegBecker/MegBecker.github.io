The Music Between Us

The Music Between Us is a podcast by James Manring about the impact and influence of music on our lives. I worked with James from the beginning to develop a look that felt classic and engaging, without leaning too heavily into one kind of music. Shown here are sketches and part of the development process, along with the finished assets.
