Adrian Piper

Adrian Piper is an American artist and philosopher whose work deals with racism, gender, and otherness, among other subjects. The book contains an illustrated essay and history of Piper's work and a record of the exhibition as it was shown at Lévy Gorvy in 2017.

Designed at McCall Associates.
